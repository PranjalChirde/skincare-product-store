How to Take the Perfect Photo

A clear, well-lit photo helps our AI give you the most accurate skin analysis.

☀️

Natural Lighting

Sit near a window with natural light facing you — avoid harsh shadows or flash.

🧘

Front-Facing, No Filters

Look straight at the camera without glasses, filters, or heavy makeup applied.

📐

Full Face Visible

Make sure your forehead, cheeks, nose, and chin are all clearly visible in frame.
